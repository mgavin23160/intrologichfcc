Syllogism Diagramming Practice

Next to each syllogism below is an interactive Venn diagram template. Toggle between the "shade" and "X" modes as needed, and click the appropriate place on the diagram to alter it. Once you've filled in the diagram, you can check it by clicking the appropriate button below the syllogism. If you make a mistake, you can click the reset button to start over. (An undo button is in the works.) Once the diagram is correct, you'll be asked whether the syllogism is valid or invalid.
